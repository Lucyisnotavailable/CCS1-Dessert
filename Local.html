San's Rival is a famous pastry and dessert shop in Dumaguete, renowned for its delectable and layered San’s Rival Cake. This delightful confection is characterized by its crisp and buttery layers, complemented by rich icing and chopped nuts, making in a beloved s weet indulgence in the coastal city of Dumaguete.
